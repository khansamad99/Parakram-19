RULES

Both boys and girls team. Each team shall consist of

1 Team Size Maximum 5 players for boys team and Maximum 3 players for girls team

2 All the matches will be played in the pattern of total 3 matches A team will have to win atleast 2 out of the 3 matches in order to win the matches will be played in the following order

Girls: 1st singles 1st doubles 2nd singles
Boys 1st singles 2nd singles 1st doubles 3rd singles 2nd doubles

3 Each of the matches will be played as a set of 3 le Each singles and each doubles will be played as a best of 3 matches

4 Three players are allowed to play one singles and one doubles only. No player can play more than one singles or more than one doubles

5 Matches will be of 21 points Matches will be played with Yonex Nylon Shuttles.

6 A player will not be allowed to play unless he has Non-Marking Shoes Matches will be played in indoor court

7 The latest general rules of Badminton World Federation shall be applied

8 The decision given by the match official will be the final decision

9 Maximum teams to be entertained will be 8 for both boys and girls
