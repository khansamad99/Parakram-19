RULES

Both boys and girls team. Each team shall consist of

1. Team Size: Maximum 4 players for boys team and Maximum 3 players for girls team.

2 All the matches will be played in the pattern of total 3 matches. A team will have to win at least 2 out of the 3 matches in order to win. The matches will be played in the following order

Girls:
1st singles 1st doubles 2nd singles
Boys:
A vs X
B vs Y
C vs Z
A vs Y
B vs X

3. The team to win first two matches will be the winner. The winner of a match is the one who wins the first THREE sets of his and the team which wins the first three games is the winner

4. A game consists of 11 points. There must be a gap of at least two points between opponents

5 Standard ITTF rules will be followed

6 Maximum teams to be entertained will be 7 for both Boys and Girls.
