RULES

Both boys and girls team. Each team shall consist of

1 No more than twelve (12) team members entitled to play, including a captain

2. A coach and, if a team wishes, an assistant coach

3 A maximum of three (3) team followers who may sit on the team bench and have special responsibilities, eg manager, doctor, physiotherapist, statistician, interpreter, etc

4. FIBA rules applicable

5 10 minutes each quarter with 2 minute break between the quarters and 5 minutes at half time (unless otherwise stated)

6 Teams should report to the basket ball court half an hour before their match All the players of the team should have same color of jersey with printed numbers. Use of abusive or foul language during the play can lead to technical fouls or expulsion depending on the referee and the organizing committee

7. The decision given by the ch official will be considered as the final decision

8 Maximum teams to be entertained will be 7 for both Boys and Girls

9 Any disciplinary violation by any member of the team will lead to disqualification of the team
