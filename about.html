ABOUT PARAKRAM
GAME | LIES | WITHIN

PARAKRAM is the annual sports-fest of Jaypee University of Information Technology, Solan; held each year with great zeal and enthusiasm. In this era of computerization and globalization where our lives have become so stressful, sports and games not only provide a gateway for recreation, but they also impart values like discipline, responsibility, self-confidence, sacrifice and accountability. This is exactly what the PARAKRAM aims to achieve with it's motto #GameLiesWithin.

On 4th October, 2019 our university will host hundreds of participants from various esteemed institutes. We cordially invite you to witness this momentous sports festival and live up to the glory of the sports.
