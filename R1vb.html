RULES

Both boys and girls team. Each team shall consist of

1. Team Size: 12 players (6+6) (For both boys and girls)

2 Rotation will be followed in the game

3. 3 sets per match

4. Teams must have uniform jerseys with numbers on the front and the back

5. All standard FIVB rules will be followed

6 In case of any dispute during the match, the decision of the referees will be considered final

and binding

7 Any disciplinary violation by any member of the team will lead to disqualification of the team

8 Maximum teams to be entertained will be 7 for Boys and Girls
